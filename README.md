### Oil, Power, and the Geopolitics of Venezuela and Iran

Independent Geopolitical Research Project

Author: Yajvan Sauri  
Publication: ResearchGate Preprint (March 2026)

---

## Abstract

This research examines whether recent geopolitical tensions involving the United States, Iran, and Venezuela may be linked to broader strategic interests in global energy resources. Nations possessing large oil reserves often become central to international political dynamics because energy supply strongly influences economic stability, military capability, and global power distribution.

Through case studies of Venezuela and Iran, this paper explores how geopolitical strategies may sometimes align with energy security and economic interests.
